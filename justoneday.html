    ONE DAY     2024

    For my first-ever Photo Roman, I combined still images and animated elements to tell a story inspired by my own experiences. Set to the hauntingly beautiful song "Prom" by SZA, the project follows an imaginary daydream that explores the sense of freedom and whimsy I often wish for when overwhelmed by everyday responsibilities. Through carefully curated images and music, this video creates a vivid and immersive narrative that captures the enchanting feeling of daydreaming. This project demonstrates my ability to seamlessly blend photography, video editing, and storytelling to produce a compelling and emotional visual experience.

    Software Used: Canon Camera T6 - Adobe Premiere Pro - Adobe Photoshop

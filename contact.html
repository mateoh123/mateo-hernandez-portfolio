MATEO HERNANDEZ MATEO HERNANDEZ

Born and raised in Southern California, my journey into the diverse creative and technical fields I explore today has been both unexpected and rewarding. My passion for photography began at 13, armed with just an iPhone 8 Plus, capturing the world around me. Over time, that creative spark expanded into coding in multiple languages (JavaScript, HTML/CSS like this website you're viewing and Python), experimenting with VR game development, and constantly pushing myself to explore new mediums.

Currently, I’m a student at New York University’s Tandon School of Engineering, pursuing a degree in Integrated Design and Media. My ultimate goal? To leverage my multifaceted skill set to address climate change and drive sustainability initiatives. Whether through creative storytelling, innovative technology, or impactful design.

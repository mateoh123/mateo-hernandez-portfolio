    Undergraduate @ New York University: Integrated Design Media

    Born and raised in Southern California, residing in the Pasadena area. I started my photography journey at the 13, when I finally switched from my shotty iPhone SE to the brand new (at the time) iPhone 8 Plus. The new shiny portable camera opened a whole new world for me. Every day, I would make my friends pose in front of the camera during PE. I dictated the poses, the lighting, and the scenery. My continued use of photography in everyday life has given me a keen eye for image composition. Finally upgrading to a used Canon EOS Rebel T6 at 16 (my current camera), and my sketchbook of ideas, I've conceptualized and executed photoshoots and assisted yearbooks in bringing their images to fruition. Ask me anything about my archive of photos, I couldn't include on here and ill happily show you!

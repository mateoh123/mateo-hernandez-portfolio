VALENTINE 03

First Valentine Alone...
 This project marked my first deliberate exploration of colored lighting to evoke emotion. The concept was simple yet impactful: capture the feelings of rejection and loneliness on Valentine’s Day. The barren, gritty concrete backdrop paired with the isolating tones of the lighting perfectly conveyed the raw emotion I aimed to express, creating a series of images that resonate with a sense of solitude and vulnerability.

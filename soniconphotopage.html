Under Blurry Lights...
 On the final day of my freshman year, while exploring with friends, we came across a serene waterfront at the Brooklyn Promenade. Inspired by the moment's spontaneity, I experimented with the interplay of unsteady rocks, exposure, and shutter speed. The result was a series of photos that captured dynamic, light-inspired patterns—evoking a mesmerizing light show that reminded me of the vibrant energy of my friend Ava.

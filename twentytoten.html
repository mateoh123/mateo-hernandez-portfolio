I know less at 20, than I did at 10
 What does it mean when you turn 20? Are you supposed to know everything? Are you still supposed to be confused about everything around you? Inspired by grid collages and my personal feelings of being confused about everything at 20; I made a collage of those feelings. Putting together things I've held dear to myself; music, my headphones, floral patterns.

It's an all-encompassing collage of not only me at 20 but aspects of me at all stages of my life.
